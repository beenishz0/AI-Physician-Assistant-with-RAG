TITLE: Evaluation of Acute Headache
[American Headache Society ]
Key Red Flags (SNOOP):
- Systemic symptoms (fever, cancer, immunocompromise)
- Neurologic deficit
- Onset sudden (thunderclap)
- Older age (>50)
- Pattern change or progressive headache

Common Causes:
1. Tension headache
2. Migraine
3. Sinus headache
4. Medication overuse headache

Evaluation:
- Full neurologic exam
- Fundoscopy
- BP measurement
- Consider CT head if red flags present

Initial Management:
Tension headache:
- Acetaminophen
- NSAIDs

Migraine:
- NSAIDs
- Triptans
- Antiemetics

Red Flag Management:
Immediate CT head
Neurology consult
